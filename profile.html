⚡ How Elon Score works
Calculated once per completed 60-minute session.
Score = Prev + (avg WPM + avg Acc) ÷ 100 + Improvement
Improvement = max(0, SessionScore − PrevScore)

Score is frozen during a session and only
updates when the full 60 minutes completes.
