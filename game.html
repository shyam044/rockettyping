About Rocket Race — Free Online Typing Game

Rocket Race is a free, browser-based typing game where your typing speed controls a rocket ship in a head-to-head space race against AI opponents. The faster and more accurately you type, the faster your rocket flies toward the finish line.

No download, no signup required. Just start typing and compete instantly. Track your WPM (words per minute) and accuracy live as you race. Create a free account to save scores, climb the global leaderboard, and track your improvement over time.

Perfect for students, professionals, or anyone who wants to improve their keyboard typing speed while having fun. The game features multiple difficulty levels, sentence-based typing challenges, and a real-time animated race track.
