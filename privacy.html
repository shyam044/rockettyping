Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Welcome to Rocket Typing. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

What We Collect

  • LocalStorage (your preferred time/words mode and difficulty)
  • Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics (how many people use the test)
  • When you see ads: Google AdSense uses cookies to show relevant advertisements
  • Leaderboard registration (optional): If you choose to register, we collect your display name and country to show on the public leaderboard. This is entirely voluntary — you can use the typing test without registering.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google Analytics – anonymous traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense – to display non-intrusive ads

You can read Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Your Data

We do not collect your email address, IP address, or any sensitive personal information.

If you voluntarily register for the leaderboard, your chosen display name and country are stored and displayed publicly on the leaderboard. You can contact us at any time to have your leaderboard entry removed.

We do not sell any data to third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These cookies help us understand how visitors use the site and allow Google to show relevant ads. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Children's Privacy

Rocket Typing is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our leaderboard, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

If you have questions, contact us using the link in the footer.
