Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to Rocket Typing. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

🔒 Short version: We do not sell your data. We do not collect your IP address. Registration is 100% optional.

📋 What We Collect

  • LocalStorage (browser): Your preferred test mode, difficulty, and duration — stored only on your device.
  • Anonymous analytics: Page views and usage patterns via Google Analytics (no personal identifiers).
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to show relevant ads.
  • Leaderboard registration (optional): If you choose to register, we collect your display name, email address (for login only), and country (displayed publicly as a flag on the leaderboard). You can use the full typing test without ever registering.
  • Sunday Championship scores: If you compete, your WPM, accuracy, score, username, and country are stored in our database to determine the weekly winner.

🔧 Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google AdSense — non-intrusive advertising. Opt out →
  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database for leaderboards and championship scores.

🗂️ Your Data Rights

We do not collect your IP address or any sensitive personal information beyond what is described above.

If you registered for the leaderboard, your display name and country are shown publicly. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@rockettyping.com.

We do not sell any data to third parties — ever.

🍪 Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics (traffic analysis) and Google AdSense (advertising). You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or opt out via Google Ads Settings.

👶 Children's Privacy

Rocket Typing is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our platform, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions? Contact us at support@rockettyping.com.
