🎯 Why Practice Typing?

Fast typing isn't just a skill — it's a superpower in today's digital world. Studies show that improving your WPM can increase productivity by up to 20%. At Rocket Typing, we focus on building muscle memory through repetitive, engaging practice. Our generated sentences use real-world vocabulary, helping you type naturally in emails, reports, or code.

Pro Tips for Better Results

  • Posture Matters: Sit straight, keep wrists neutral, and relax your shoulders to avoid strain.
  • Accuracy Over Speed: Aim for 95%+ accuracy first — speed follows naturally.
  • Daily Drills: Spend 10–15 minutes daily. Track your consistency score to measure evenness in your pace.
  • Touch Typing: Learn to type without looking at the keyboard — it's the single biggest speed unlock.
  • Keyboard Choice: Mechanical keys can boost comfort and feedback, but start with what you have.

❓ FAQ

Q: How is WPM calculated?
We count every 5 correct characters (including spaces) as one word, divided by the number of minutes elapsed. This is the standard gross WPM formula.
Q: Do I need to sign up?
No! You can take the typing test instantly for free without any registration. Signing up is optional and only needed to save your score to the global leaderboard.
Q: What is the Sunday Championship?
Every Sunday, a special 60-second championship opens. You get one attempt. The highest scorer wins a cash prize. Results are shown in your local currency.
Q: Is this mobile-friendly?
Yes! Rocket Typing works on desktops, tablets, and phones — though a physical keyboard gives the best experience.
Q: Can I see my country's flag on the leaderboard?
Yes! When you register, select your country and your flag emoji will appear next to your name on all leaderboards.
